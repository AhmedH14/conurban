Consolidated Urban Property Holdings

Consolidated Urban Corporation is a property investment company incorporated in South Africa. Our focus of activity is the acquiring of properties mainly entire City Blocks in Johannesburg CBD which are close to major transport nodes or University campuses. We readapt the use of these properties to residential and retail with the view to controlling the areas in which we operate.

About us

We are a self-funded company with no corporate debt. We do have facilities ie. either first mortgages or construction facilities on individual properties. In all of our property holdings we are the managing partner. Our major focus of activity is the acquiring of distressed properties and turning these properties into revitalized well performing accretive assets. Our portfolio comprises of affordable residential and student apartments of which we have over 1000 units and a combination of big box retailers, a mix of line shops and large commercial tenants. Our senior debt finance comes from a number of funders in the South African banking sphere. Our management company, Consolidated Urban Management implements state of the art technology that manages all rental collections, maintenance, cleaning and security issues. All our buildings have Biometric entry systems. Our occupancy ratio across the residential and commercial portfolio year on year is 100% and our bad debts sit at between 0.8% - 1.2%. Our construction company, Consolidated Urban Construction specialises in refurbishing old residential buildings and converting existing defunct office space into residential units. Our turnkey refurbishments are unique in their offering as we have managed to develop a unique product over the last 12 years.
